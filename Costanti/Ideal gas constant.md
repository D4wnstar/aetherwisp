The **ideal gas constant** is a constant found in the [[Ideal gas|ideal gas law]]:
$$\begin{align}
R=N_{A}k_{B}&=8.315\ \text{K/J} \\
&=1.986\text{ cal/K} \\
&=0.0821\text{ l}\times \text{atm/K}
\end{align}$$
$N_{A}$ is the [[Avogadro number]] and $k_{B}$ is the [[Boltzmann constant]].
